**1\. Standard Applications**

*(Documents attached to on-boarding email)*

* Facility Application  
  * Signed Quotation  
* StratCol Contract  
* ABSA 6995 Form  
* Last 3 Invoices (if applicable)  
* Existing Mandates (if applicable)  
* Risk Analysis (existing users only)  
* Beneficiary Application *(only if split payments apply)*  
* Bank Guarantee *(if option selected)*

---

**2\. Standard FICA Requirements**

**2.1 Entity Documentation**

* Company Registration Documents / Trust Deed / NPO Certificate (as applicable)  
* Tax and VAT Documents  
* Full Description of Services **or** Website Link

**2.2 Individuals (Directors / Shareholders / Owners / Members / Trustees)**

* Copy of ID  
* Proof of Residential Address (not older than 3 months or lease agreement)

**2.3 Business Information**

* Proof of Business Premises Address (not older than 3 months or lease agreement)  
* Latest 3-Month Bank Statement  
* Confirmation of Accounting Officer Letter  
  *or*  
  3rd-Party Confirmation Letter (template attached to email)

---

**3\. Additional Documents by Product Type**

**3.1 Premium Collections**

* Intermediary Agreement

**3.2 Call Centre Clients**

* COLMS Application *(compulsory)*  
* Service Agreement  
* Product Description  
* Supplier Contact Information  
* Call Script

---

**4\. Industry-Specific Required Documents**

* **NPO:**  
  * Constitution  
  * Resolution  
  * List of Board Members (Name, Surname, ID Number)  
* **Trust:**  
  * Letter of Authority  
  * Beneficiary IDs & Addresses  
  * Organogram (on request)  
* **Body Corporate:**  
  * Resolution  
  * List of Board Members (Name, Surname, ID Number)  
* **Insurance PC:**  
  * FSCA Certificate  
* **Security Services:**  
  * PSIRA Certificate  
* **FIC Centre:**  
  * FIC Registration Number  
* **Loan Provider / Debt Counsellor:**  
  * NCR Certificate

---

**5\. Additional Documents (Risk-Based / If Applicable)**

* Existing Users: Instruction Letter for Changes  
* Sale of Business: Sales Agreement  
* Financial Statements (on request)  
* Usage of Consent Form (if applicable)

**6\. Mandatory Checks & Risk Scoring**

**6.1 Verification & Screening Tools**

* **ITC / Credit Check:** XDS  
* **Procure Upload:** LexisNexis  
* **Company Registration Verification:**  
  [https://www.bizportal.gov.za/](https://www.bizportal.gov.za/)  
* **ID Verification & AVSR:** EFS24  
* **SARS VAT Search:**  
  [https://secure.sarsefiling.co.za/vatvendorsearch.aspx](https://secure.sarsefiling.co.za/vatvendorsearch.aspx)

---

**6.2 Risk & Compliance Checks**

* Social Media Screening (Company & Individuals)  
* DPIP / FPIP Verification  
* Hello Peter – Complaints & Compliments Review  
* Google Maps – Business Premises Address Verification  
* FICA Risk Calculator (attached to email – mandatory)

---

**6.3 Sanctions Screening**

* OFAC / UN Sanctions Search:  
  [https://sanctionssearch.ofac.treas.gov/](https://sanctionssearch.ofac.treas.gov/)

  [https://tfs.fic.gov.za/Pages/TFSListDownload](https://tfs.fic.gov.za/Pages/TFSListDownload)  
* UN Security Council – 1267 Sanctions List:  
  [https://main.un.org/securitycouncil/en/sanctions/1267/aq\_sanctions\_list](https://main.un.org/securitycouncil/en/sanctions/1267/aq_sanctions_list)
